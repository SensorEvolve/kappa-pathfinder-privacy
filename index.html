Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2024

Introduction

Kappa Speed Run ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices for the Kappa Speed Run mobile and web application.

Privacy First: Kappa Speed Run does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data Collection

Kappa Speed Run does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, the app:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Does not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or location
  • Does not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • Does not store any data on external servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored exclusively locally on your device using standard browser localStorage mechanisms. This data includes only:

  • Your quest completion checkmarks
  • App preferences (filters, settings)
  • Player level selection

No personal or identifying information is stored. You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser data or uninstalling the application.

Third-Party Content

Kappa Speed Run displays images and content related to the game Escape from Tarkov. These images are loaded from tarkov.dev, a community-run resource for Tarkov data.

When images are loaded, your device makes requests to tarkov.dev servers. These requests may include standard web information (IP address, user agent) but are not tracked or logged by our application. For information on tarkov.dev's data practices, please refer to their website.

No Advertising

Kappa Speed Run does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising services or SDKs. No advertising-related data collection occurs.

Children's Privacy

Kappa Speed Run does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app requires no personal information to use and is safe for all ages. However, Escape from Tarkov is rated M for Mature (17+), and this companion app is intended for players of that age group.

Data Security

Since Kappa Speed Run does not collect, store, or transmit personal information, there is no personal data at risk. All app functionality operates entirely locally on your device. Your quest progress data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Your Rights

As we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear all app data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the application
  • Use the app without any account or personal information
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns

International Users

Kappa Speed Run can be used worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns. All data remains on your local device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Noted in the app update description when applicable

We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
